What next?

You may reconnect to your home wifi and return to the SimpleAQ website, and wait up to 10 minutes to see if you start receiving data.

If it doesn't work, try the following:

  1. Unplug your device and plug it back in.
  2. Try disconnecting from the SimpleAQ wifi network.
  3. Connect to the SimpleAQ wifi network, navigate to http://192.168.4.1, and check your wifi credentials.
  4. If you are unable to unplug your device and plug it back in, go back to settings and select "Submit Changes and Reboot" again to force reboot.
  5. If all else fails, go to the settings page at http://192.168.4.1 and check the simpleaq service logs for hints.
