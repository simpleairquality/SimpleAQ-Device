What next?

You may reconnect to your home wifi and return to the SimpleAQ website and wait 10 minutes to see if you start receiving data.

If it doesn't work, try the following:

  1. Unplug your device and plug it back in.
  2. Connect to the SimpleAQ wifi network, navigate to http://192.168.4.1, and check your wifi credentials.
  3. If all else fails, go to the settings page at http://192.168.4.1 and check the simpleaq service logs for hints or errors.
